Bottom Line

With Atlas Flex, the app comfortably supports 2–3 crews of 30–50 members each (~$30–73/mo). The Flex tier's 500-connection limit and 500 ops/sec IOPS cap are the primary constraints. Upgrading to Atlas M10 (~$57/mo) is the single highest-impact change — tripling connection capacity to 1,500 and removing the IOPS ceiling, enabling ~225 concurrent users.
